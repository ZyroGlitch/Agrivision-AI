🌿 Welcome to AgriVision AI

Your partner in agricultural innovation

AgriVision AI is a powerful web platform that uses advanced Artificial Intelligence to transform the way agricultural images are analyzed. Built for farmers, researchers, and agricultural professionals, it offers a seamless workflow for uploading crop or plant images and selecting from three machine learning algorithms Convolutional Neural Network (CNN), K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN) and Support Vector Machine (SVM).

How AgriVision AI Works

  • 🔢 Choose your preferred AI model: CNN, KNN, or SVM.
  • 🖼️ Upload a clear image of a flower, fruit, or vegetable.
  • 🧠 The selected algorithm analyzes the image and makes a prediction.
  • 📊 See the predicted class, confidence score, and algorithm used.
